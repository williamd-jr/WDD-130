Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make seafood less fishy?

To reduce the fishy taste, soak seafood in milk for 30 minutes before cooking, or use citrus like lemon to mask the odor.

What are the best seafood choices for beginners?

Some great beginner-friendly seafood options include shrimp, tilapia, and salmon. These are easy to cook and flavorful.

Can I substitute frozen seafood for fresh seafood?

Yes, frozen seafood can be substituted for fresh. Just make sure to properly thaw it before cooking.
