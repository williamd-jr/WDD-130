Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to cook shrimp?

How do I store fresh seafood?

Fresh seafood should be stored in the coldest part of your refrigerator, ideally in a container with crushed ice. It's best to use it within a couple of days.

Can I freeze seafood?

Yes, most seafood can be frozen. Make sure to store it in airtight packaging to avoid freezer burn.

Is seafood healthy?

Yes! Seafood is packed with protein, omega-3 fatty acids, and other essential nutrients, making it a healthy choice for your diet.

Can I use frozen seafood in recipes?

Yes, you can use frozen seafood in most recipes. Just be sure to thaw it properly before cooking.
